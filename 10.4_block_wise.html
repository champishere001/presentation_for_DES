Block 3 (Continued)
Household Consumer Expenditure
Monthly and annual expenditure details
💰 Expenditure Questions (Ask for amounts in Rupees)
Q3.5.1 (A)
What is the usual consumer expenditure in a month for household purposes out of purchase of goods and services excluding footwear, clothing etc?
Example: ₹23,000
Include: Regular monthly expenditure, recurring expenses (telephone, electricity bills apportioned monthly)
Exclude: Durable goods, unusual expenditures (ceremonies, hospitalization), clothing/footwear
Q3.5.2 (B)
What is the imputed value of usual consumption in a month from home grown stock like rice, cereals, pulses, vegetables, milk, firewood, chips, cow dung, etc?
Example: ₹0
Record at ex-farm/ex-factory price if home grown stocks used regularly
Q3.5.3 (C)
What is the imputed value of usual consumption in a month from wages in kind, free collection, gifts, etc?
Example: ₹3,000
Include: Free electricity, telephone, medical services, food-grains, meals, mid-day meals (at local retail prices)
Q3.5.4 (D)
Expenditure on clothing, footwear during last 365 days
Example: ₹20,000
Q3.5.5 (E)
Expenditure on household durables during last 365 days
Example: ₹4,50,000
Auto-Calculated (Q3.5.6): Usual monthly household consumer expenditure = [A + B + C + (D+E)/12]
Example: [23000 + 0 + 3000 + (20000+450000)/12] = ₹65,167

Block 6
Current Weekly Activity Particulars
Reference Week: Last 7 days preceding the survey date
📅 Reference Week Structure
Important: Collect information for each day of the reference week (7 days). For each household member, record activity status, hours worked, industry, and earnings.
Day 1 (1st day): Most recent day of the reference week
Day 7 (7th day): Earliest day of the reference week
📝 Information to Collect for Each Day
For Status Codes 11-51
Hours actually worked
Example: 8 hours
For Status Codes 61,62,71,72
Hours would have worked
Example: 8 hours (if worked)
Total Hours Actually Worked (Item 7.1)
Considering all work performed during the day
Sum of all working hours for the day
Hours Available for Additional Work (Item 8.1)
If available for additional work, hours available for such work during the day
Example: 0 (if not available)

Block 6 (Daily Entry)
Daily Activity Entry Format
Items 3.1 to 3.7 - Day-wise particulars
📝 Questions to Ask for Each Day
For each day of the reference week, ask:

"Did [Name] do any work yesterday/the day before for pay, profit or family gain?"

If YES: "How many hours did [Name] work?"
If NO: "Why didn't [Name] work? Was it due to sickness, other reasons, or was there no work available?"
Item 5.1 / 5.2
Industry (2-digit NIC-2025 code)
